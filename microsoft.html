The Birth of Microsoft

How Microsoft Was Founded

In 1975, Gates and his friend Paul Allen saw an opportunity to develop software for the emerging market of personal computers.

Meeting Paul Allen

Gates and Allen met during high school, where they bonded over their shared interest in computers. This led to the eventual founding of Microsoft.

Key Developments

In the early years, Microsoft developed software for IBM computers, which quickly gained popularity.
