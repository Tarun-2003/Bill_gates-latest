Introduction to Bill Gates

Overview of Bill Gates

Bill Gates is a renowned entrepreneur and philanthropist, best known for co-founding Microsoft.

Early Life and Education

Bill Gates was born on October 28, 1955, in Seattle, Washington. From a young age, he showed an interest in computing, which led him to pursue programming.

Important Milestones

In 1975, Gates co-founded Microsoft with Paul Allen, which would become one of the most successful tech companies.
